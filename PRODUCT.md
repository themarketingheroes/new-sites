# PRODUCT.md — Florida Demolition Experts website

## Register
**Brand.** This is a marketing site. The design IS the product.

## Product purpose
Convert South Florida homeowners, contractors, developers, and flippers into free on-site quote requests for demolition, site preparation, concrete removal, and earthwork. Primary conversion is a phone call (CallRail-tracked 954-444-6643) or the on-site quote form. Secondary goal: positioning Florida Demolition Experts as the premium, trusted choice over commodity demo crews.

## Users
1. **Homeowners** doing a tear-down, pool removal, interior gut, or storm-damage cleanup. Anxious. Have heard horror stories about cheap crews. Need permits handled.
2. **General contractors and flippers** who have been burned by demo crews leaving messes and missed schedules. Need a reliable partner that does not require babysitting.
3. **Developers** with land-clearing and grading needs on bigger commercial sites. Value timeline certainty and one in-house team.

## Tone
Confident without bragging. "We pull every permit." "Site swept clean. Every job." Never apologetic, never salesy, never folksy. Skews industrial-premium. Treats the reader as someone with a real job to do, not a lead to extract.

## Anti-references (avoid these aesthetics)
- **Generic demolition company sites**: red/black "rugged" corporate vibe with stock photos of bulldozers. Looks like every other commodity demo crew.
- **"Demolition Gods"** (a competitor that literally copied FDE's old site). New site must feel categorically different.
- **AI-generated landing-page template look**: rounded cards in a grid, gradient text, "We provide quality services since 20XX" copy. Anything that telegraphs "AI made this."
- **Glassmorphism for its own sake**, hero-metric template, identical card grids, em dashes anywhere.

## Strategic principles
1. **Premium over cheap.** Every page should make it obvious FDE is the choice when the price-shopper crew has failed you.
2. **Founder forward.** Nataliya is the brand. Her photo, her quote, her name, her phone number, on every page.
3. **Proof over claim.** Real job-site photos. Real testimonials with cities. Real video. License number visible. No stock imagery.
4. **One call to action.** Free on-site quote. The phone number 954-444-6643 is always visible. Forms are short.
5. **Mobile is primary.** A South Florida homeowner standing in their yard looking at the structure they want gone is the canonical mobile user. Sticky phone CTA, large tap targets, fast load.
